Craniofacial Measures Orientation

Zygomatic-Zygomatic (Zy-Zy): Mediolateral length of skull. Maxilla facing superiorly, measure from verticalis

Basion-Bregma (Ba-Br): Superoinferior length of cranium. Skull lateralis, measure from posterior

Euryon-Euryon (Eu-Eu): Mediolateral length of cranium. Skull verticalis, measure from superior

Glabella-Opisthocranion (Gl-Op): Anteroposterior length of cranium. Skull lateralis, measure from superior

Gonion-Condylion (Go-Co): Superior Superoinferior length of mandible. Condyles superiorlym, measure from lateral

Gonion-Gonion (Go-Go): Mediolateral length of mandible. Teeth inferiorly, measure from superior

Pogonion-Gonion (Po-Go): Anteroposterior length of mandible. Teeth inferiorly, measure from lateral

Alveolare-Nasion (Al-Na): Superoinferior length of face. Skull lateralism, measure from anterior
