Humerus

Humeral length: Maximum linear distance, from proximal limit of humerus to distal limit.

Humeral head mediolateral breadth: Linear distance of most medial and lateral limits of humeral head, not including tuberosities.

Humeral head anteroposterior length: Linear distance of most anterior and posterior limits of humeral head

Humeral distal end mediolateral breadth: Linear distance from medial epicondyle to lateral epicondyle

Humeral distal end anteroposterior length: Linear distance of most anterior and posterior limits of distal end.

Humeral diaphysis circumference: Measure at most lateral point of deltoid tuberosity

Epiphyseal Fusion

0) Not available

1) Open, no fusion

2) Partially fused

3) Completely fused
