Tibia

Tibial length: Maximum linear distance, from the proximal limit of the intercondylar eminence to the distal limit of the medial malleolus.

Tibial proximal end mediolateral breadth: Linear distance of medial limit of medial epicondyle to lateral limit of lateral epicondyle.

Tibial proximal end anteroposterior length: Linear distance of most anterior limit to the most posterior limit

Tibial diaphysis circumference: Measure across shaft nutrient foramen

Epiphyseal Fusion

0) Not available

1) Open, no fusion

2) Partially fused

3) Completely fused
